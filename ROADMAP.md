### Development Milestones

#### M1 — Knowledge Transfer Foundation

**Goal**

Establish a reproducible knowledge-transfer workflow for graduating researchers.

**Deliverables**

* Knowledge Transfer workflow.
* B — Knowledge Transfer Checklist completed by the graduating researcher.
* C — Verification Report completed by the responsible incoming student.
* D — Acceptance Form completed by the advisor.
* Daily research and meeting templates.

**Definition of Done**

* The graduating researcher prepares the complete transfer package.
* The responsible incoming student independently reproduces the MRR.
* The advisor reviews the evidence and approves the transfer.
* Feedback is incorporated into the next revision.

#### M2 — AI-assisted Research Workflow

**Goal**

Use AI to improve research quality while preserving independent thinking.

**Deliverables**

* AI Research Coach.
* AI Daily Self-review.
* AI-assisted documentation review.
* AI-assisted issue review.

#### M3 — Research Contribution System

**Goal**

Establish a fair, transparent, and reproducible contribution framework.

**Deliverables**

* Contribution categories.
* Contribution portfolio.
* Recognition guidelines.
* Contribution evaluation.

### Long-term Vision

The Open Research Playbook aims to help every laboratory build research that is:

* Easy to understand.
* Easy to reproduce.
* Easy to transfer.
* Easy to continue.
