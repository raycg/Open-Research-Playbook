# Open Research Playbook

> **An engineering handbook for reproducible research, knowledge transfer, and AI-assisted research.**

**We don't just build research.**

**We build research that others can understand, reproduce, and continue.**

---

## Why?

Research is more than publishing papers.

A research project is truly complete only when another researcher can:
- Understand it
- Reproduce it
- Improve it
- Continue it

The Open Research Playbook aims to build a research culture that values:
- Independent thinking
- Rigorous verification
- Clear documentation
- Effective knowledge transfer

---

## Core Philosophy

Every research activity should follow four fundamental principles.
🧠 Think
🔬 Verify
📝 Document
🤝 Transfer

> **Research is not complete until someone else can continue it.**
