# Knowledge Transfer Checklist

> **Has the graduating researcher prepared everything required for knowledge transfer?**

This checklist is completed by the graduating researcher before
verification begins.

---

## Why?

Knowledge transfer should provide everything required for another researcher to understand, reproduce, and continue the research.

Only essential and reproducible research assets should be transferred.
