# Graduation Knowledge Transfer Acceptance Form

> **Has knowledge transfer been completed correctly, and may the
> graduating researcher leave the laboratory?**

This form is completed by the advisor after reviewing the Knowledge
Transfer Checklist and Verification Report.

## Why?

The graduating researcher may leave only after the transferred research
has been independently verified and accepted by the advisor.

## What?

### Review

| Item | Result |
|---|---|
| Knowledge Transfer Checklist (B) completed | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Verification Report (C) completed | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Defense-slide experimental results reproduced | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Repository and research assets accessible | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Remaining work documented | ☐ Yes ☐ No |
| Responsible incoming student ready to continue | ☐ Yes ☐ No |

### Advisor Decision

- [ ] Approved — the graduating researcher may leave.
- [ ] Conditionally Approved — corrections are required.
- [ ] Not Approved — knowledge transfer is incomplete.

> Conditional approval does not authorize departure until the advisor
> confirms that all required corrections are complete.

| | | |

> is complete, the responsible incoming student has reproduced the MRR,
> and the advisor has approved the transfer.**
